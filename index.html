Welcome to My Research Page

I am a PhD student at EPFL-ETHZ, focusing on the intersection of robotics and arts in education. My work involves developing innovative educational tools like Sthymuli, a circular robot equipped with various modules such as a vibration plate, tentacles, or theatrical components. By integrating sensory feedback and creative expression, I aim to enhance learning experiences and foster a deeper engagement with STEAM subjects among diverse teacher and student populations.
